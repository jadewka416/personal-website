Bookabite

← Back to UX/UI Projects

Summary

Mission

Bookabite is a high-fidelity mobile app prototype created for people with special dietary requirements or assistance. By implementing filters with different dietary requirements or catering needs to search for restaurants and catering services, it eases user pain point in unable to find restaurants that serve their needs and provide suitable assistance.

Development process

I identified there is a lack of inclusive platform to make custom requests at restaurants, and how it creates frustration in customers with special needs to dine at any local restaurants. E.g. a person with reduced mobility would likely have issues with entering restaurant without accessible pathway or assistance, that greatly reduced their choice of dining at any local restaurants. Therefore, I implemented different filters to search for restaurants that match their dietary requirements and custom needs.

Target audience

People with special dietary needs and require additional assistance
