Golf for Cancer

← Back to UX/UI Projects

Summary

Mission

The mission of Golf for Cancer is to bring golf enthusiasts together at fundraising events to support research into Breast Cancer, Prostate Cancer and All Cancer Types. In 2023, 69 Golf for Cancer events took place across the country, raising more than $700,000 to support the Canadian Cancer Society's (CCS) investment into lifesaving research, compassionate support programs and advocacy work to shape a healthier society.

My Contributions

I bridged the gap between raw data and visual design to create a more engaging fundraising platform. I began by utilizing GA4 and DonorDrive to extract behavioral insights, specifically analyzing user interaction patterns and attention span via heatmaps to identify friction points in the donation funnel. Using these findings, I developed a comprehensive set of new user stories that informed my transition from low-fidelity wireframes to polished high-fidelity prototypes. I strategically integrated new feature, e.g. improving site's mobile responsiveness to ensure a seamless experience for donors on any device, leveraging keywords to boost SEO.
