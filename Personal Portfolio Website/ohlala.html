Summary

Background

Ohlala is a high-fidelity mobile app prototype project created for the Google UX Design certificate. Ohlala is a grocery store website, the store theme is organic, eco-friendly, and ethically-sourced fresh products.

Development process

I identified there is a lack of inclusive platform to purchase organic, ethically-sourced products from local farmers. So I created a prototype where customers can purchase these products from their local farmers online and delivered to their home.

Target audience

Customers who want organic, ethically-sourced products

Main Focuses

  • UX/UI Design
  • User Journey
  • inclusiveness
