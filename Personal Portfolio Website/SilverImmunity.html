SilverImmunity

← Back to CX/Customer Experience Projects

Summary

Mission

SilverImmunity is an awarded project at Microsoft HK JobHack Hackathon 2021. The core mission of SilverImmunity is to "Unlock the power of technology for the Elderly". It aims to provide essential healthcare information and support to senior citizens, who are particularly vulnerable to health crises like COVID-19 due to higher fatality rates and existing health conditions. The project seeks to bridge the digital divide, enabling the elderly to access hygiene practices, medical support, and daily necessities safely through technology.

Development process

The project followed a structured development lifecycle: Research and Problem Identification: I identified that while the elderly are the most vulnerable, they often lack proper hygiene knowledge and face difficulties using complex digital tools. Technological Integration: The solution leverages Azure Cognitive Services, specifically utilizing the LUIS (Language Understanding) API to power a conversational AI (Chatbot). Implementation Phases: The development timeline was estimated at 5–6 months with a team of four experts. The process involved building an AI capable of understanding natural language to simplify user interactions. Operational Planning: Beyond technical development, the process included planning for 24/7 technical support and collaborating with NGO partners to provide social worker assistance.

Target audience

Primary Users: Elderly individuals in Hong Kong, particularly those aged 70 or older, who require healthcare support and information.
Secondary Users: Disabled individuals and patients with chronic illnesses who share similar vulnerabilities.
Support Network: NGO partners and committed volunteers who assist in recruiting users and providing manual help for services like online grocery ordering.
